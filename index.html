    My UX Skills

    User Research

    I approach user research in 2 ways: qualitative and quantitative. Both interesting, and both equally important.
    I’m a good listener, and I like people. More specifically, users who have thoughts and feelings. That’s where the greatest insights and solutions come from.

    UI Design

    UI comes naturally to me, as my background spans over 15 years of graphic design, branding and creative direction.
    I’ve worked on small brands, and I’ve worked on big ones.
    I’ve been designing style guide since I graduated, so this lends itself well to UXUI.

    User Empathy

    I take an empathetic approach on all projects. I have opinions, sure, but what really matters is what you think.
